about

The PinkTrio is a group of young jazz musicians in San Diego, California founded in 2015 after meeting at various jazz camps and performing at the San Diego House of Blues. PinkTrio specializes in classic and modern jazz tunes. Our mission is to sustain and bring jazz to future generations like ourselves.

The PinkTrio is available for banquets, restaurants, hotels, country clubs, private parties, convention and conference entertainment, fund raisers, corporate functions, wedding receptions and other special occasions. We also perform with additional guest performers including vocalists, horns, saxophone and other instruments to suit your needs.

Our music is the perfect choice for a tasteful unique classy musical backdrop for parties or dinner. Live music adds an element of excitement and our unique young group is a hit at any event.
